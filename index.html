Progressive Cobweb App

Add spooky stickers to your photos!

Your photos won't be uploaded anywhere and will remain private (unless you choose to share them yourself later!)

This is a starting point for a PWA by the @samsunginternet team! Follow the instructions here.

Tap on your photo to add stickers and tap here when you're done!

To change your sticker, see the top of script.js

You're done! Tap and hold or right-click on the image to save it.

If you like, you could tweet it with the #mozfest hashtag!
